## 1 What’s a legal memo used for? | 法律备忘录的用途是什么？

The purpose of a legal memo is to inform, not to argue the facts. A legal memorandum serves as an objective, stand-alone document that identifies the risks and any unknown facts that need further investigation. Unlike a [legal brief](https://pro.bloomberglaw.com/insights/litigation/how-to-write-a-legal-brief/) presented to the court, a legal memo isn’t the place to offer a legal opinion. It should maintain an impartial tone, with no implied preference for one side or the other. 法律备忘录的目的是提供信息，而不是争辩事实。法律备忘录是一份客观的、独立的文件，用于识别风险以及需要进一步调查的任何未知事实。与提交给法庭的[法律诉状 (legal brief)](https://pro.bloomberglaw.com/insights/litigation/how-to-write-a-legal-brief/)不同，法律备忘录不是提供倾向性法律意见的地方。它应保持不偏不倚的语气，不暗示偏袒任何一方。

Your memo must, therefore, provide an objective summary of all relevant case law and how it applies to the facts at hand. Don’t cherry-pick relevant cases to cite; the omission of negative case law only compromises future legal strategy and heightens client legal exposure. 因此，你的备忘录必须对所有相关判例法及其如何适用于手头的事实提供客观的总结。不要只挑选有利的相关案件来引用；遗漏负面判例法只会损害未来的法律策略，并增加客户的法律风险。

## 2 Types of legal memos | 法律备忘录的类型

Legal research memos can come in many forms – from broad 50-state surveys to more nuanced research on a particular point of law. Generally, legal memos fall into one of two main categories, closed or open, depending on the scope of the case law you’re being asked to apply to the matter at hand. 法律研究备忘录有多种形式——从广泛的50州法律调查到针对特定法律点的更细致的研究。通常，法律备忘录分为两大类：封闭式或开放式，这取决于要求你应用于当前事项的判例法范围。

### 2.1 Closed legal memo | 封闭式法律备忘录

A closed legal memo is written with a focus on specific case law or other primary law. For closed memo assignments, you’ll be given the relevant case law to be used in your writing. 封闭式法律备忘录侧重于特定的判例法或其他主要法律。对于封闭式备忘录任务，你将获得用于写作的相关判例法。

### 2.2 Open legal memo | 开放式法律备忘录

Open legal memos are far more challenging than closed legal memos. You’ll need to research and identify the relevant case law yourself, investigate and analyze the most legally significant facts involving the client, and provide a critical assessment of how the court may apply the law to that matter. 开放式法律备忘录比封闭式法律备忘录更具挑战性。你需要自己研究并找出相关的判例法，调查并分析涉及客户的最具法律意义的事实，并对法院将如何将法律应用于该事项提供批判性评估。

## 3 Standard legal memo template | 标准法律备忘录模板

Standard legal memos are generally written using the Issue, Rule, Application, and Conclusion (IRAC) format: 标准法律备忘录通常使用“争议点、规则、应用和结论”（IRAC）格式编写：

- **Issue:** Identify the problem. **争议点 (Issue)：**明确问题。
    
- **Rule:** State the relevant law. **规则 (Rule)：**陈述相关法律。
    
- **Application:** Apply the law to the facts and develop arguments. **应用 (Application)：**将法律应用于事实并展开论证。
    
- **Conclusion:** Identify the strongest arguments and next steps. **结论 (Conclusion)：**确定最有力的论点和下一步行动。
    

Use the template below as an outline for your legal memo: 使用以下模板作为你法律备忘录的大纲：

### 3.1 Heading or caption | 标题或抬头

A section, titled “Memorandum,” identifies: 标有“备忘录 (Memorandum)”的部分需注明：

- The recipient (To: _______) 收件人 (To: _______)
    
- The author (From: ____) 作者 (From: ____)
    
- The assignment submission date (typically in MM DD YYYY format) 任务提交日期（通常为 月 日 年 格式）
    
- The subject of the memo (Re: __________) 备忘录主题 (Re: __________)
    

### 3.2 Issue: question presented | 争议点：提出的问题

This brief one-sentence statement defines how the law applies to the legal question at hand, and the jurisdiction where the matter will be decided. The question presented is specific and impartial and doesn’t assume a legal conclusion. 这个简短的单句陈述定义了法律如何应用于手头的法律问题，以及该事项将在哪个司法管辖区裁决。提出的问题应当是具体的、不偏不倚的，并且不预设法律结论。

### 3.3 Rule: brief answer | 规则：简要回答

This is a quick-hit legal prediction of the outcome to the question presented, based on a short (four- to five-sentence) explanation that references relevant law and facts. 这是对所提问题结果的快速法律预测，基于引用相关法律和事实的简短（四到五句）解释。

### 3.4 Application: statement of facts | 应用：事实陈述

A concise, impartial statement of the facts captures the heart of the legal matter, as well as current and past legal proceedings related to the issue. The facts can be grouped chronologically or thematically, whichever format presents the facts most clearly. 简洁、公正的事实陈述抓住了法律事项的核心，以及与该争议点相关的当前和过去的法律程序。事实可以按时间顺序或主题进行分组，以最能清晰呈现事实的格式为准。

### 3.5 Application: discussion | 应用：讨论

This restates the main facts and the overarching legal rule. Several paragraphs outline the various legal topics to be addressed in the case and provide an analysis of the legal issues, usually ordered in subsections. 这部分重申主要事实和首要法律规则。用几个段落概述案件中要解决的各个法律主题，并提供对法律问题的分析，通常分小节排列。

### 3.6 Conclusion | 结论

The assigning attorney will likely read this section first. It predicts how the court will apply the law and describes how confident you are in your prediction based on the data. With an impartial advisory tone, you identify the next steps and propose a legal strategy to proceed. 分配任务的律师很可能会先阅读这部分内容。它预测法院将如何应用法律，并描述你根据数据对自己的预测有多大信心。以客观的建议语气，确定下一步行动并提出推进的法律策略。

## 4 How to write a legal memo | 如何撰写法律备忘录

If you tackle your memo assignment using the recommended format and legal memo template above, you’ll be more likely to find an appreciative supervising attorney, deliver a better work product, reduce the number of revisions required to arrive at a final product, and, most importantly, please the client. 如果你使用上述推荐的格式和法律备忘录模板来处理备忘录任务，你将更有可能获得指导律师的赞赏，交付更好的工作成果，减少最终定稿所需的修改次数，最重要的是，让客户满意。

### 4.1 Review assignment materials | 审查任务材料

Whatever the memo format – closed or open – it’s important that you fully understand the scope of the assignment before you start typing. This way, you won’t waste time researching irrelevant case law and can focus your memo on the specific legal questions and jurisdictions that will be most beneficial to the case. 无论备忘录格式是封闭式还是开放式，在开始撰写之前完全理解任务的范围都至关重要。这样，你就不会把时间浪费在研究不相关的判例法上，而是可以将备忘录的重点放在对案件最有利的特定法律问题和司法管辖区上。

### 4.2 Locate secondary sources for background | 查找二手资料作为背景

Secondary sources are a great starting point for your legal memo research, including: 二手资料是你法律备忘录研究的一个极好起点，包括：

- Books (书籍)
    
- Treatises (专著)
    
- Law review articles (法律评论文章)
    
- Legal analysis publications (法律分析出版物)
    
- [Practical Guidance](https://pro.bloomberglaw.com/products/legal-research-and-software/practical-guidance/) (实务指南)
    

Although secondary legal sources aren’t binding, meaning courts aren’t required to follow these sources, they’re still helpful resources when you’re first familiarizing yourself with a legal issue. However, remember that while you may know little about a topic, the same may not be true of your audience. Those who will read your legal memo, such as law partners and assigning attorneys, will already know general law. Understand your audience’s presumed level of knowledge and then identify the most mission-critical questions to address. These knowledge gaps will inform your fact-finding and research. 虽然二手法律资料没有约束力（意味着法院不需要遵循这些资料），但当你初步熟悉法律问题时，它们仍然是很有帮助的资源。然而，请记住，虽然你可能对某个主题知之甚少，但你的受众可能并非如此。那些将阅读你法律备忘录的人（如律所合伙人和分配任务的律师）已经了解一般法律。要了解你的受众预期的知识水平，然后确定需要解决的最关键的问题。这些知识差距将为你的事实调查和研究提供方向。

Use secondary resources to better fill in the main legal topics and issues as they relate to the facts in the legal memo assignment. Your legal research should help frame the issue and lead to other relevant materials, including cases and statutes. Make sure to vet all sources for relevance and authority. 使用二手资料更好地填补与法律备忘录任务中事实相关的主要法律主题和问题。你的法律研究应该有助于构建问题的框架，并引出其他相关材料，包括案例和法规。确保审查所有来源的相关性和权威性。

### 4.3 Identify primary case law | 识别主要判例法

Primary sources such as court opinions can often be found using research tools, but relevant case law isn’t always immediately apparent. In such cases, work your way backward. Reviewing secondary sources can help you identify a list of relevant primary sources, including case law and related statutes. 像法院意见这样的主要来源通常可以使用研究工具找到，但相关的判例法并不总是显而易见的。在这种情况下，可以逆向推进。审查二手资料可以帮助你确定相关主要来源的列表，包括判例法和相关法规。

### 4.4 Develop a research plan | 制定研究计划

As you come across key primary sources, create a research plan to keep them organized. Your research plan should list the relevant primary law and how the case or statute relates to your comprehensive legal analysis. 当你遇到关键的主要来源时，创建一个研究计划以使它们保持条理。你的研究计划应列出相关的主要法律，以及案件或法规如何与你的综合法律分析相关联。

### 4.5 Validate your legal research | 验证你的法律研究

Once you have your research plan, verify all your research to make sure you’re relying on the most current case law available. Although a legal memo is written for internal stakeholders such as the assigning attorney, and not for the court system, it may nonetheless serve as a primer for future case material. Attorneys may later incorporate any case law citations within the legal memo into court filings in support of their arguments. 一旦有了研究计划，就要验证你所有的研究，以确保你依赖的是现有的最新判例法。虽然法律备忘录是为分配任务的律师等内部利益相关者撰写的，而不是为法院系统撰写的，但它仍然可能作为未来案件材料的基础。律师日后可能会将法律备忘录中的任何判例法引用纳入法庭文件中，以支持他们的论点。

Given this broad potential reach, it’s imperative to verify all case law within your legal memo. Any unverified case law that later makes its way into public documents will result in an admonition from the court. 鉴于这种广泛的潜在影响，验证法律备忘录中的所有判例法是必不可少的。任何未经验证的判例法如果后来进入公开文件，都将招致法院的警告。

For each cited case, make sure you know the following: 对于每个引用的案件，请确保你了解以下内容：

- **Composite analysis:** The overall treatment of the cited case by other courts **综合分析：**其他法院对被引案件的总体处理态度
    
- **Direct history:** How a cited case has moved through the court system **直接历史：**被引案件在法院系统中的审理过程
    
- **Case analysis:** Cases that have subsequently cited the case **案件分析：**随后引用过该案件的案例
    
- **Authorities:** Cases relied on by the court in the cited case **权威依据：**法院在被引案件中所依赖的案例
    
- **Citing documents:** Legal materials, such as court opinions, administrative decisions, and secondary sources that reference your case by citation **引用文件：**通过引用提及你的案件的法律材料，如法院意见、行政决定和二手资料
    

### 4.6 Write an objective analysis | 撰写客观分析

Legal memos showcase your critical legal thinking skills. Use your research plan and research materials to help organize your analysis. Remember to clearly state the law and the facts, in the active voice, and present your analysis in a logical manner. 法律备忘录展示了你批判性的法律思维能力。利用你的研究计划和研究材料来帮助组织你的分析。请记住，要清晰地使用主动语态陈述法律和事实，并以合乎逻辑的方式呈现你的分析。

Use legal memo space wisely. Remember, legal memo length varies by subject. Some topics require only a short summary, while others compel long-form treatment. For guidance, search your firm’s office document management system for previous legal memos. 明智地使用法律备忘录的篇幅。请记住，法律备忘录的长度因主题而异。有些主题只需要简短的总结，而其他主题则需要长篇的论述。如需指导，可以在你律所的办公文档管理系统中搜索以前的法律备忘录。
